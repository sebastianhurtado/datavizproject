Looking for ET

The words selected were funding, exoplanets and SETI (Search for Extraterrestrial Intelligence). The purpose of the project was to find out if the announcement of the discovery of exoplanets, led to increased coverage regarding the funding for the search for extraterrestrial intelligent ventures, between 1999-2017. From the 100,291 articles with the word funding published in The Guardian since 1999, only 214 referenced SETI, 112 referenced exoplanets, 13 articles mentioned Funding & SETI together, Funding & Exoplanets were mentioned together in 9 articles, and only 8 articles referred to Exoplanets & SETI. Hence, the coverage of the discovery of Exoplanets did not have major impact in the coverage of funding activities to SETI by The Guardian.
